How to make Liberian Fufu

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Plantains
  • Cassava

    • Fufu is a West African Dish that is served with Pepper Soup or any soup of your choice.

    Instructions:

      1. Cut the Plantains and Cassava into small pieces
      2. Place Plantains and Cassava into a blender
      3. Blend until you get a smooth texture
      4.After it's blended mix with water
      5. Put the mixture into a bowl and place into microwave for atleast 5-10 mins
      6.Take out of microwave and wait for it to cool down so you can enjoy! :)
